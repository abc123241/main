Artists Page

Atif Aslam

Atif Aslam is a renowned Pakistani playback singer, songwriter, and actor known for his soulful voice and emotional singing style. He gained popularity with songs like “Tera Hone Laga Hoon” and “Jeene Laga Hoon,” which became huge hits in Bollywood. His unique vocal texture and ability to express deep feelings have made him one of the most loved singers across India and Pakistan. Atif Aslam has also received several awards and continues to perform in concerts worldwide.

Shreya Ghoshal

Shreya Ghoshal is one of the most celebrated Indian playback singers, known for her melodious voice and versatility. She started her career after winning a singing reality show and quickly rose to fame with songs like “Bairi Piya” and “Teri Ore.” Shreya has sung in multiple languages including Hindi, Bengali, Tamil, and Telugu. Her classical training and emotional depth make her songs timeless and highly admired.

Arijit Singh

Arijit Singh is a highly popular Indian singer known for his heartfelt and soulful songs. He became famous with the song “Tum Hi Ho,” which made him a household name. Arijit is known for his emotional expression and versatility, singing romantic, sad, and energetic tracks. His simplicity and dedication to music have earned him a massive fan following

Armaan Malik

Armaan Malik is a young and talented Indian singer and songwriter known for his smooth voice and modern music style. He has sung popular songs like “Bol Do Na Zara” and “Main Rahoon Ya Na Rahoon.” Armaan is also recognized internationally and has released English songs, making him a global artist. His dedication and versatility have made him popular among the youth.

Diljit Dosanjh

Diljit Dosanjh is a famous Punjabi singer, actor, and performer known for his energetic music and acting skills. He has given many hit Punjabi songs like “Do You Know” and has also acted in Bollywood movies. Diljit’s unique style, lively performances, and connection with fans have made him a global icon in Punjabi entertainment.

Play 90s Songs

The 1990s era is widely regarded as one of the golden periods of Bollywood music. Songs from this decade are remembered for their melodious tunes, emotional lyrics, and beautiful compositions. Even today, these songs continue to be popular among music lovers of all generations.

During the 90s, many talented singers, lyricists, and music directors created songs that became evergreen classics. Their creativity and unique musical style made the songs memorable and enjoyable for listeners. These songs are still played in homes, radio stations, and music playlists.

One of the special features of 90s music is its simplicity and emotional depth. The lyrics often express feelings of love, friendship, happiness, and sadness in a very meaningful way. Because of this, listeners can easily connect with the songs and enjoy them for a long time.

Listening to 90s songs brings a strong feeling of nostalgia. Many people remember their childhood memories, movies, and special moments associated with these songs. This emotional connection is one of the main reasons why 90s music remains timeless.

This playlist is created to celebrate the beauty of 90s music and allow listeners to enjoy unlimited songs from that era. By exploring this playlist, music lovers can experience the charm, melody, and unforgettable magic of classic Bollywood music.
